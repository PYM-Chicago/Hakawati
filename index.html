About Hakawati

PYM Chicago is launching Hakawati: our first-ever Arabic school coming to the southwest suburbs this summer. Students will receive 7 courses over 4 weeks at beginner or intermediate levels. Classes are taught by experienced local Arabic professors and focus on 3amiyya (colloquial) Arabic.

Hakawati students will develop stronger conversational skills, grow confidence in speaking Arabic, and explore cultural connections. Follow our social media @PYMCHICAGO for updates and announcements.

Applications are now closed. We look forward to seeing you this summer!
