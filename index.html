About Our School

Welcome to Hakawati Arabic School, dedicated to teaching Arabic language and culture to students of all ages. Our mission is to preserve and promote the Arabic language while fostering cultural understanding and appreciation.

We offer professional instruction in Modern Standard Arabic (MSA) and various dialects with experienced teachers who are passionate about education.

Our Programs

  • Beginner Arabic: Perfect for those starting their Arabic journey
  • Intermediate Arabic: Build on your existing knowledge
  • Advanced Arabic: Master conversational and written Arabic
  • Cultural Classes: Learn about Arab traditions, history, and culture
  • Private Tutoring: One-on-one personalized instruction
