ABOUT

PYM Chicago is launching Hakawati: our first-ever Arabic school coming to the southwest suburbs this summer! This school is going to provide you with 7 courses over 4 weeks of beginner or intermediate level Arabic courses. These courses will be taught by experienced local Arabic professors and teachers who will focus on 3amiyya (colloquial) Arabic. Hakawati students will develop a deeper understanding of the Arabic language and become more comfortable speaking the language conversationally. More information about the school will be announced through our social media @PYMCHICAGO or on this page: Bit.ly/HAKAWATI

Anyone between the ages of 18-30 can apply with priority given to Arabic heritage speakers. If you are interested please fill out this application by Sunday May 3rd!
